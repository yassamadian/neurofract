Evidence Standards

Tier 1 sources are published in Nature, Science, PNAS, Nature Reviews Neuroscience, or equivalent journals (impact factor >10). All assessment tools cited are validated instruments with published psychometric properties. All intervention protocols are supported by at least one peer-reviewed primary study or systematic review. No claim in Neurofract's product literature exceeds what the cited evidence supports. Every user-facing recommendation includes its source in accessible plain language.
