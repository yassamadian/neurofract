The Science

Three pillars of cognitive health

Every Neurofract protocol is built on validated neuroscience — the same fractal patterns that govern your brain's architecture, made accessible for everyday life.

🧠

Neuroplasticity

Structured cognitive exercises trigger long-term potentiation, rewiring neural circuits for faster processing and stronger associative memory.

⚡

Neuromodulation

Evidence-based breathwork and neurofeedback protocols modulate key neurotransmitter systems — dopamine, acetylcholine, and GABA — to optimize arousal state.

🔄

Recovery & Consolidation

Sleep and rest architecture is as important as training. Our protocols time interventions to circadian windows where consolidation peaks.
